MacBook M4


The Apple MacBook Pro with the M4 chip, launched in late 2024, represents a significant advancement in performance and efficiency. Available in 14-inch and 16-inch models, these laptops feature the M4, M4 Pro, and M4 Max chips, offering configurations with up to a 16-core CPU and a 40-core GPU. They come standard with 16GB of unified memory, expandable up to 128GB, and storage options up to 8TB SSD. The Liquid Retina XDR displays support up to 1600 nits brightness for HDR content, and the new nano-texture glass option reduces glare

Additional enhancements include a 12MP Center Stage webcam, a six-speaker audio system with spatial audio, and extended battery life of up to 24 hours. Connectivity is robust, featuring Thunderbolt 5 ports, HDMI up to 8K, Wi-Fi 6E, and Bluetooth 5.3. The MacBook Pro M4 models run on macOS 15.1, incorporating advanced AI features.
